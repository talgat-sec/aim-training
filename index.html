How Score Works

Your score is built from two kinds of clicks:

— accurate clicks are direct hits on targets

— free clicks are extra clicks between targets

Both categories add to your total score.

The goal is not only accuracy, but also overall click activity, so you can warm up your hand and get up to speed before a game.
